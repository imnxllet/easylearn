Tours

Edgecation offers tour options for you to maximize your learning.

Creative and New Media Camp

With our Creative and New Media Camp, we challenge our students to engage with media in new and engaging ways. The landscape of media communication is rapidly changing and with this camp, students will learn the importance and usefulness of new media. These classes will take place at the University of Toronto Mississauga campus. By the end of this camp, students will be able to produce pieces of new media that explore new ways of communicating information.

FOCUS OF THE CAMP:

1. An introduction to communication and media theory
2. Creative assignments
3. Learning different industry practices across different countries
4. Improve speaking and writing skills in English 

Duration/Audience

2 weeks / 18 or above

Leadership development summer camp

With our leadership development summer camp, students will have the chance to develop their professional leadership and networking skills with the help of our specialized workshops that include engaging and interacting with industry professionals through networking events.

In our leadership development summer camp, students also will have an immersive cultural experience by exploring the many different attractions and venues that the city of Toronto has to offer.

CAMP HIGHLIGHTS:

1. Unique cultural learning experience
2. Understanding the differences between "managing" and "leading"
3. Understanding different leadership strategies and methods
4. Networking with industry professionals

Duration/Audience

2 weeks / 18 or above

Branding strategy summer camp

Over the course of 2 weeks, students on this tour will have the opportunity to learn branding strategies in University of Toronto's Rotman Commerce School of Management. This branding strategy workshop will be taught by Professor Claire Tsai, with an emphasis on real-world examples and implementations of brand strategies.

By the end of this summer camp, students will have met and networked with industry professionals, along with a new understanding of developing brand strategies.

FOCUS OF THE CAMP:

1. Develop a clear understanding of brand strategies.
2. Gain industry insights in finance, accounting, and marketing.
3. Unique cultural learning experience
4. Engaging with others to improve oral communication skills

RECOMMENDATION:

This camp requires students to have some background of english as the course will be taught in english. The camp best fits students who wish to grow in industry that is concerned with consumer goods.
