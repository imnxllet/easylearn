What We Do

At Edgecation, we aim to give international students the opportunity to have a unique cultural and educational experience in Canada. From attending classes taught by university professors to exploring everything else Canada has to offer. We aim to provide a wholesome experience that gives international students a taste of what it feels like to be an active student in Canada

By joining our tours, you will attend specialized workshops run by industry professionals with a focus on professional development. Additionally, students will also be brought to various attractions and events around the city to experience Canada outside an academic environment.

Our Team

Hugo Chang: Operations and logistics

As the director of operations, Hugo leverages his expertise and knowledge to help international students feel accommodated and welcomed in Canada. Over the last 3 years, Hugo has been growing student clubs in the University of Toronto such as the UTICU (University of Toronto International Cantonese Union) that help international students adjust to their new environment. Hugo recognizes the unique challenges that international students commonly face when coming into a new country, and has helped many students build their confidence to overcome these challenges.

From his experience managing clubs as well as planning orientation events for international students, Hugo manages the majority of operations and logistics at Edgecation.
