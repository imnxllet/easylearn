Student Residence

The university of Toronto, downtown currently has 11 student residences which can accommodate up to 6572 students. Scarborough and Mississauga campus hold 767 and 1577 students, respectively. Most of these residences include a cafeteria which provides more than 3 meals a day(depends on the meal plan the student chooses). Other amenities such as Wifi, laundry, study room, computer labs and etc.
