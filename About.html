What Edgecation does

At Edgecation, we aim to give international students the opportunity to have a unique cultural and educational experience in Canada.

 From attending classes taught by university professors to exploring everything else Canada has to offer. We aim to provide a wholesome experience that gives international students a taste of what it feels like to be an active student in Canada.

By joining our tours, you will attend specialized workshops run by industry professionals with a focus on professional development. Additionally, students will also be brought to various attractions and events around the city to experience Canada outside an academic environment.

Our Team

Talents make Edgecation.

Chris Lu: 

Technical and Finance

Over the years, Chris has been using his skills in computer science and actuarial science to actively assist multiple clubs at the University of Toronto. At Edgecation, Chris oversees the majority of tech-related tasks as well as promotional material. Chris believes that international students are in need of additional care and support while students adjust to their environment. Chris is determined to be the driving force that ensures international students are able to access the same opportunities as any other student in Canada.

Foura Li:
Operations and Logistics

Over the years, Foura has taken on numerous tasks to generate leads and sponsors to assist multiple clubs at the University of Toronto. Coming to Canada at the age of 16, Foura understands the challenges students may face when adjusting into a new environment. Foura leverages on her skills in interpersonal relationships as well as her experience in broadcasting to develop leads and promote the sales of Edgecation.

Natalie Deng:
Sales and Marketing

As an international student herself, Natalie uses her skills in communications and marketing to coordinate with administrative bodies to accommodate the needs of international students. Having founded her own club at the University of Toronto with 800 members, Natalie manages the administrative and marketing duties at Edgecation. Natalie hopes to bridge Chinese and Canadian cultures in a way that helps international students transition into Canada smoothly.
