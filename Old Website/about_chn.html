Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the best experience for students who wish to explore different parts of the world through study tour. Students will not only learn the difference in culture and lifestyle, they can also get an impression of the industry at which they are interested outside of their country. We hope to lead students into discovering their true interests with intact course content while providing them the opportunity to develop valuable relationships aboard.

Benefits of coming to Canada

Recent years, the new government administration in USA made Canada a relatively attractive place for Chinese VISA students. The number of international students show an increase of 83% since 2008 and 10% comparing to previous year. According to CBIE, 33% of the international students in Canada are from China and more than half of the international students apply for permanent residence after study. The positive and inviting image of Canada has become a favourable place for not only immigrants but student who wishes to pursue high quality education. There are number of benefits in coming to Canada when compared to the USA.

  • Open access to the job market
  • Strong government support
  • The cheaper tuition
  • Short school terms for job opportunity
  • Simple study visa process
  • Cultural diversified
