What We Offer


Prestige learning experience

This tour will provide the best lecturer for the students in the tour. These lecturers are the qualified and talented professors from prestigious universities. Classes will be taken place in the respective university which can enhance the learning experience for the students. High-end teaching instruments such as conference style classroom with outlet to each seat. Students will also get to receive valuable feedback from the lecturers that can benefit them in their critical thinking and problem solving skills. The content of the course will be based on real world applications and students will have chance to do presentations or experiments that supplement their study. Resources at the university are also opened to students; places like the library, study rooms and listen in lectures.

24 hours support and monitor

The tour will provide 24 hours support for the students. Our team leaders will be ready in the case of emergency or resolve any problems the students may have. We will provide a travel sim card during the tour for each students. This will allow students to have access to the internet and contact their family if needed. Guarantee the safety of the students will be our top priority for the whole tour and we will work hard to ensure both the quality and the well-being of the students.

Connection to company

The tour will expose students to various industries in Canada which allow students to gain knowledge on the industry that interest them. The visits will allow students to ask question regarding to the industry or the company. It can help students to develop relationships with professionals that can the potential asset to the students in his or her career path. We recognize the importance of networking and we believe students should start as early as possible to equip themselves with the work place. These associations will also help student to see a clear picture of the work which they want to do for their future.



connection with local students

The tour will offer opportunities for students to meet with both local and international students. We believe that meeting with local students can help students understand the culture and the value in Canada. Meeting with the international students can allow students to see the life they might have if they choose to study in Canada. These chances will be provided throughout the tour.

Personalized tours

The tour will be scheduled in a way that will maximize the benefits student can get from it. For example, if the students are interested in the subject of art, the tour will consist a lot of art galleries or workshops from local artists. For business students, company tour and high quality case studies are one of the many opportunities we offer.

Interactive with Forever Learning from website or APPS

Individual wechat group and social media platform will be used throughout the trip to ensure quality. Students can provide requests on the spot or after hours. All announcement regarding to the trip will be transparent to all students and parents.



Provide certificate and report card in the end

Students will receive a certification from the instructors upon finishing the course. The certificate can help recognize what the students had accomplished during the tour. The report card will also be offered in the last class to better measure the commitment of the students. The report cards are constructed by the lecturer which is highly knowledgeable and influential in their field of study.
