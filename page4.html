Why Edgecation

We provide the best service in every tour.

PRESTIGE LEARNING EXPERIENCE

With Edgecation, students will meet some of the university's top lecturers. These instructors are qualified and talented professors from the University of Toronto. The courses will be taught at respective university campuses in classrooms with the best learning facilities that enhance the overall student learning experience. All course content will be based off real-world application and practices. By the end of the workshop, students will receive valuable feedback from the instructors meant to help students with their professional development.

24 HOURS SUPPORT AND MONITOR

The tour will provide 24 hours support for the students. Our team leaders will be ready in the case of emergency or resolve any problems the students may have. We will provide a travel sim card during the tour for each students. This will allow students to have access to the internet and contact their family if needed. Guarantee the safety of the students will be our top priority for the whole tour and we will work hard to ensure both the quality and the well-being of the students.



BUILDING CONNECTIONS

With our tours, students will have the chance to build connections and interact with different industry professionals. With these networking opportunities available, students will have the chance to ask questions and engage in conversations that will give them more insight into various industries. With these tours, we aim to help students discover their interests and help make the necessary connections that will help them be successful.




INTERACT AND MEET WITH LOCALS

Students who attend our tours will also have the opportunity to build relationships with local students. As part of the cultural learning experience, we believe that it is important for our students to meet and get to know local students. We believe that an authentic cultural experience involves interacting with other people from cultures that are different from your own.


PERSONALIZED TOURS

At Edgecation, all our tours are highly specialized to the kinds of tour packages that we provide. All our tours are organized in ways that optimize learning from both indoor and outdoor environments. These personalized tours include company tours, gallery visits, and many more.





INTERACTIVE AND DEDICATED ONLINE PAGES

Our dedicated page on Wechat and other social media platforms (coming soon) allow us to remain within reach for anyone who wishes to connect with us. With our social media pages, we are able to answer questions, respond to requests, and post all our tour updates in real time. This helps us be honest and transparent to the public. During our tours, our online team will be readily available to keep parents informed and updated on the tour at all times.

CERTIFICATE AND FEEDBACK REPORTS

Upon completion of our workshops, students will receive a certificate as well as individual feedback reports from the instructors. The certificate recognizes the completion of the course, and the feedback report highlights all the positive improvements that the student has exhibited throughout the tour. Additionally, these feedback reports are a useful tool to help students to continue improving even after the tour.



SUPPORTED BY UNIVERSITY OF TORONTO PROFESSORS

Along with the support from University of Toronto professors, we provide an authentic and prestigious learning experience for our students. With our classes and workshops taught at the University of Toronto (unless specified in the tour package), we strive to provide an authentic and encompassing learning experience within a university environment. We believe that our tour packages will provide helpful and insightful experiences for students who are considering to further their studies.

FOCUS ON SOFT SKILL DEVELOPMENT

Throughout our camps, students will engage in numerous activities that will help them develop their soft skills such as speaking, listening, and overall networking skills. By socializing and meeting new people, we encourage all our students to engage and develop their abilities by practicing using their soft skills.
