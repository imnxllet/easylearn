Our Tours


Leadership development summer camp

Details:

The camp will provide the best experience in leadership development through workshop and networking events. City exploration opportunity will be provided throughout the camp to allow students to fully understand the culture of Canada. The camp will focus on city exploration and workshops to enhance the experience, therefore, no course will be provided.

Duration:

2 weeks

Target audience:

18 or above

Focus of the camp:

1. Importance of understanding the culture
2. Difference between management and leadership
3. Six type of leadership
4. Understanding the basic of different business industry.





Branding strategy summer camp

Details:

The camp consists a 2 week course constructed by the Rotman Commerce professor, Claire Tsai. The students will gain an understanding of how branding strategy works across all industries. Students will be given the opportunity to review different industries by networking with professionals that are currently in the field.

Duration:

2 weeks

Target audience:

18 or above

Accomodation

Student residence

Focus of the camp:

1. build understanding of branding strategy
2. gain insights into industries such as finance, accounting, marketing
3. allow students to explore a new culture
4. improve on their english oral skills

Recommendation:

This camp requires students to have some background of english as the course will be taught in english. The camp best fits students who wish to grow in industry that is concerned with consumer goods.





Creative and New Media Camp

Details:

The intro to communication and media camp explores how the basic building blocks of communication come together to create meaning. This culminates in an assignment where the students will purposefully create a piece of media that conveys a message accurately and succinctly. The course will take place at the University of Toronto Mississauga campus, giving students another perspective on their possible student experience aboard.

Duration:

2 weeks

Target audience:

18 or above

Focus of the camp:

1. Introduction to communication and media theory
2. Creative assignments
3. Industry difference between China and Canada
4. Improve listening and writing in English
