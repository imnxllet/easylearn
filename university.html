University of Toronto

In UofT, $1.3 billion dollars of revenues are from foreign students and the number is expected to climb even higher next year. There are currently 17,452 international students and over 10,000 of them are from China. This signals the potential it has for having study tour in Toronto. The growing population of Chinese students interested in Canadian university will bring tremendous amount of revenues to the business.


The reputations for some of the Canadian universities are not far behind from some of the IVY league universities in USA. According to several liable ranking, Times Higher education; Shanghai Jiao Tong; US News Best Global University and NTU Ranking, UofT stayed top 20 for the past years. The variety of undergraduate programs will also pinpoint student’s talents and interests. There are currently 700 undergraduate programs in UofT. These programs cover humanities, commerce, physic and mathematic, life science, computer science, engineering, music and art and physical education. In addition, the cost of tuition and the cost of living in Toronto are more affordable to students when compared to USA, therefore, Toronto must be an attractive place for both parents and students in China.

University of Waterloo

Waterloo university is one of the top universities in Ontario that focuses on accounting, engineering and architecture. The famous Blackberry Inc. and the computer retailer giant, Canada computer was founded in this university. From where it is located, waterloo is called the silicon valley of Canada. Students will enjoy a being in a high-tech city where innovations never stop. The university offers co-op programs to their students. The co-op programs will not only bring exposure to students who are interested in the specific field, it also allow students to earn up to $45,000 to $80,000 a year. To international student, this is the opportunity for them to both learn and enjoy a saving in a foreign country. It connects students with more than 300 companies ranging from small to large Multi-national companies.

McGill University

McGill university is a public research university in Canada that bears the name of “Harvard of the North”. It is located in one of the most beautiful cities in Canada, Montreal. The university offers more than 300 different programs with some of the highest average requirement. There are a total of 142 Rhodes Scholars and 12 Nobel prize winners that graduated from McGill. The medical-doctoral program is ranked first place in Canada for 13 consecutive years. The business program, IVEY, is also one of the best business program in Canada. The tuition for international student is around $40,000CAD, depending on the program. The cost of living in Montreal is around $16,000CAD a year if purchase meal plan from the school.
