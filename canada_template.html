Overview of Canada’s market

The number of international student enrolling in post-secondary education in Canada continues to grow for the last 17 years. Applications of post-secondary in 2013 is around 160,000 and the number almost doubled in 2015 at 236,000. This goes to tell investors that that more students are looking to receive their education in Canada. In fact, the amount of international student in Ontario’s universities grew 88.5% in the last six years. In addition, the national image of Canada is staying positive in the foreseeable future which will directly impact the amount of students wanting to enter the country. More students and parents are viewing Canada as a good option to live and study. According Maclean Canada, Canadian on average have better living standard than USA. The average income in Canada is estimated to be CAD $36,000 while USA is estimated to be CAD $35,300. The country is also more culturally diversified, making it better for VISA students.

The Market of study tour

The market of study tour has never been better. More parents are seeking ways to broaden their children’s point of view through other educational channel. Study tour is one of the major ways to do that. Looking at the statistic from 2015, the number of people participated in a study tour reached 500,000 and it is expected to increase in the next 5 years. The market of study tour can be viewed as a complement for the number of chinese student studying abroad. The additional incomes will allow families to explore different ways of providing the best education for their children. According to pinchain.com, China is now the biggest “exporting” country for international students with a market growth of 40% since 2015. The estimated market size is approximately RMB $19 billion dollars in 2016, therefore, we are expecting to see a lot of people going abroad to explore their education option. In fact, a study from EY consulting reported that 69% of the people will want to join a study tour to open their perspective and learn about other cultures, following the options of obtaining higher quality education. Study tour is a wonderful way for students to spend their summer. By exploring the different culture, students will become more socially conscious and improve upon their morality.

Benefit of Coming to Canada


Recent years, the new government administration in USA made Canada a relatively attractive place for Chinese VISA students. The number of international students show an increase of 83% since 2008 and 10% comparing to previous year. According to CBIE, 33% of the international students in Canada are from China and more than half of the international students apply for permanent residence after study. The positive and inviting image of Canada has become a favourable place for not only immigrants but student who wishes to pursue high quality education. There are number of benefits in coming to Canada when compared to the USA.



Open access to the job market

In USA, students are only allowed to take part time jobs that are specific to their field of study, however, this is not the case for Canada. International students in Canada are free to work in any part time job while continue on with their study.

Strong government support

Students are allowed to work during their study. After graduating, they can obtain work permit which allow them to stay up to 3 years in the country. This is beneficial for students who want exposure to the industry and earn experience from multinational companies.

The cheaper tuition

The average tuition for international students in USA is higher than in Canada. Students are expected to pay on average $33,000CAD in USA for one year while in Canada, it’s $25,000.



Straightforward university application process

The application process in Canada is more straightforward and transparent in Canada. Most of the programs in Canada emphasize on marks and this is an advantage for international students since most of them put much focus on their schooling.

Short school terms for job opportunity

The average academic term in Canada university runs from September to April. This shorter than in USA which is on average September to May. Students in Canada enjoy more time in searching for internships or spend more time going back to their country.

Simple study visa process

It is easier to get study visa in Canada than USA. USA application process depends a lot on the interview while in Canada, submitting the right documents is enough.
